Why you should join Yale

Yale University is one of the world’s most prestigious and historic institutions, known for its rigorous academics, influential alumni network, and deeply rooted traditions. Whether you're interested in law, medicine, the arts, public policy, or the sciences, Yale provides an environment that encourages intellectual exploration, leadership, and innovation, all while fostering a strong sense of community and purpose.

Inter-school Competition JULY 16, 2024

A coveted British Parliamentary (BP) style high school tournament also run by the YDA. It attracts elite teams preparing for international BP events.

The Yale–Harvard football rivalry, known simply as The Game, is one of the oldest and most celebrated rivalries in American college football. First played in 1875, it has become much more than a sporting event—it's a historic tradition filled with pageantry, pride, and spirited competition. Held annually in late November, The Game alternates between Yale’s iconic Yale Bowl and Harvard Stadium, drawing thousands of students, alumni, and fans from around the country. For many, it’s the highlight of the fall semester, uniting the campus in a display of school spirit unlike any other. Whether you're cheering from the stands, tailgating with classmates, or watching from afar, The Game represents a legacy of rivalry, respect, and shared tradition that has endured for nearly 150 years.A coveted British Parliamentary (BP) style high school tournament, run by the Yale Debate Association (YDA), draws elite teams from across the country and around the world. It reflects Yale’s commitment to fostering global debate excellence at the high school level.
